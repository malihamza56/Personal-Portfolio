# 📖 About

Welcome to my personal portfolio website.

This portfolio has been designed and developed to present my work, technical skills, projects, achievements, and learning journey as a Computer Science student and aspiring Backend Developer.

Rather than being just a static website, this portfolio represents my continuous growth as a developer. Every section has been carefully structured to provide visitors with an overview of my technical abilities, ongoing projects, and passion for software development.

Whether you're a recruiter, developer, client, or fellow learner, this portfolio offers a quick insight into who I am and what I build.
